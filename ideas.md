# Latin Kitchen Leicester — Design Brainstorm

## Approach 1 — Mercado Vivo (Street Market Energy)
<response>
<text>
**Design Movement:** Neo-Brutalist Latin Street Art
**Core Principles:**
- Raw, expressive typography with heavy contrast
- Layered textures evoking hand-painted murals
- Asymmetric, collision-based layouts
- Vivid, unapologetic colour

**Color Philosophy:** Saffron yellow (#F5A623), chilli red (#D63B2F), deep charcoal (#1A1A1A), and cream (#FAF0E6). Colours drawn from Mexican market stalls — warm, sun-baked, energetic.

**Layout Paradigm:** Overlapping card stacks, diagonal section dividers, text that bleeds off edges. Content blocks collide and layer rather than sit in clean grids.

**Signature Elements:**
- Hand-drawn border accents and rough underlines
- Torn-paper section transitions
- Bold condensed display font contrasting with a clean sans-serif body

**Interaction Philosophy:** Hover states feel physical — cards tilt, buttons stamp. Micro-animations echo the energy of a busy market.

**Animation:** Entrance animations use staggered slide-ins from different directions. CTA buttons pulse with a warm glow. Section reveals use clip-path wipes.

**Typography System:** Bebas Neue (display/headlines) + DM Sans (body). Tight letter-spacing on headlines, generous line-height on body.
</text>

## Approach 2 — Cocina Moderna (Warm Editorial)
<response>
<text>
**Design Movement:** Contemporary Latin Editorial
**Core Principles:**
- Warm, food-magazine aesthetic
- Generous whitespace with bold typographic anchors
- Photography-first layout
- Earthy warmth balanced with sharp contrast

**Color Philosophy:** Deep terracotta (#C1440E), warm ivory (#FDF6EC), forest green (#2D5016), and gold (#D4A017). Inspired by clay pots, jungle foliage, and golden corn — grounded and appetising.

**Layout Paradigm:** Full-bleed photography sections alternating with editorial text columns. Asymmetric two-column hero. Menu items presented as magazine feature cards.

**Signature Elements:**
- Serif display font for headlines, creating editorial gravitas
- Thin horizontal rules as section separators
- Circular image crops for social proof portraits

**Interaction Philosophy:** Smooth, confident transitions. Scroll-triggered reveals. Hover on food cards lifts and brightens.

**Animation:** Fade-up entrance animations with slight Y-offset. Parallax on hero image. CTA button has a warm fill sweep on hover.

**Typography System:** Playfair Display (headlines) + Source Sans 3 (body). Italic accents for subheadings.
</text>

## Approach 3 — Sabor Urbano (Bold Urban Energy) ← SELECTED
<response>
<text>
**Design Movement:** Urban Latin Fusion — bold, kinetic, conversion-optimised
**Core Principles:**
- High-contrast, punchy visual hierarchy driving eyes to CTAs
- Diagonal geometry and angular cuts creating forward momentum
- Vibrant accent colours on a dark base for premium street-food feel
- Every section earns its place through conversion intent

**Color Philosophy:** Flame orange (#FF6B2B), golden yellow (#FFD23F), deep espresso (#1C1008), warm white (#FFF8F0). Orange = energy and appetite; yellow = warmth and value; dark base = premium, not cheap. CTA buttons always in flame orange.

**Layout Paradigm:** Dark hero with diagonal clip-path transition into lighter sections. Staggered feature cards. Full-width CTA strips. Sticky header with persistent CTA button.

**Signature Elements:**
- Diagonal section dividers (clip-path polygon)
- Bold number/stat callouts in oversized type
- Pill-shaped trust badges with icon + text

**Interaction Philosophy:** CTAs have a warm pulse animation. Cards lift on hover with subtle shadow. Form fields glow orange on focus.

**Animation:** Hero text slides in from left with stagger. Section reveals use fade-up with 0.2s delay per element. Sticky header fades in on scroll.

**Typography System:** Syne (bold display headlines, geometric and distinctive) + Nunito Sans (body, warm and readable). Headlines in uppercase for impact.
</text>
